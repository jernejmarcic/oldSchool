### Moderated caucus about the benefits and drawback on the medicinal use of Marijuana 
*10 minutes, 1 minute per speaker*

"The delegation of Ireland believes that the Medicinal use of Marijuana  has many benefits as long as the use is moderated and monitored closely by doctors in addition to other treatments and if more standard treatments have failed. As of writing this document, the Medical Cannabis Access Program has approved the use of cannabis for the following conditions:
-   Spasticity associated with multiple sclerosis
-   Intractable nausea and vomiting associated with chemotherapy
-   Severe, refractory (treatment-resistant) epilepsy.

However, more conditions can be approved on a case-to-case basis."
